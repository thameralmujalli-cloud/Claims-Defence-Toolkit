Claims Defence Toolkit

Test the gates. Track the clock. Decide the path.

Three tools for the owner's side: a Claim Defence Test that walks every claim through the five substantiation gates, a 28-Day Clock Calculator for any notice date, and a Settle vs Escalate Matrix for the decision that matters most.

Walk a specific claim through the five gates. A claim that fails any gate is dead at that gate. A claim that clears all five is one that has to be settled or arbitrated on its merits.

GATE 01NOTICE

Was the notice issued within 28 days of the contractor becoming aware of the event giving rise to the claim?

GATE 02PARTICULARS

Were the supporting particulars (detailed claim, evidence, time and cost impact) submitted within the contractual window (typically 42 to 84 days)?

GATE 03CAUSATION

Is the link from the event to the alleged time or cost consequence properly evidenced? Does the event actually impact the critical path or cost baseline as claimed?

GATE 04QUANTUM

Is the time or cost claimed properly calculated, with itemised, invoice-level evidence? Or is it a headline figure without substantiation?

GATE 05MITIGATION

Did the contractor take reasonable steps to mitigate the time or cost impact, and is that mitigation documented?
